SEATTLE - Jazz Alley will host a benefit for the Edmonds Community College Kobe Relief Fund at 7:30 p.m. Feb. 13 at the nightclub, 2033 Sixth Ave.,  Seattle.     The college's big band and the vocal jazz group Soundsation '95 will be featured, with special guests Don Lanphere on tenor sax, Allen Vizzuti on trumpet and the Chuck Metcalf Octet.     The minimum donation is $10 for adults and $6 for students. Parking is free in the garage behind Jazz Alley. Proceeds will benefit the survivors of the earthquake in Kobe, Japan, where the college has a campus.     For more information, call 640-1650. For reservations, call Jazz Alley at 441-9729
