WASHINGTON - Recovery workers have found the bodies of an American contractor and his Taiwanese wife who were killed in last week's earthquake in Turkey, the State Department said yesterday.     Philip Fedorchak, who worked for naval architects Gibbs and Cox, and his wife, Hsaio-mi, died under the rubble of their apartment building in the Sea of Marmara town of Golcuk, the site of a big naval base. Their ages and home towns were not immediately available.
