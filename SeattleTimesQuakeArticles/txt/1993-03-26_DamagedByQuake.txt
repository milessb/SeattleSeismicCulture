OREGON DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION WORKERS INSPECT AND PHOTOGRAPH THE UNDERSIDE OF A BRIDGE ON HIGHWAY 18 NEAR DAYTON, ORE., AFTER IT WAS DAMAGED BY THE EARTHQUAKE YESTERDAY. THE BRIDGE, CONSTRUCTED IN 1957, WAS CLOSED AND MAY TAKE WEEKS TO REPAIR. THE QUAKE, ESTIMATED AT 5.3 TO 5.7 ON THE RICHTER SCALE, PRODUCED SCATTERED DAMAGE IN THE WILLAMETTE VALLEY, INCLUDING CRACKS IN THE CAPITOL BUILDING IN SALEM AND THE PARTIAL COLLAPSE OF A SCHOOL IN MOLALLA.
