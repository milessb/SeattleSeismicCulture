Moderate quake hits    north-central Alaska    --  PALMER, Alaska        A moderate earthquake registering 5.5 on the Richter scale rocked north-central Alaska yesterday,  but officials said there were no reports of damage        Bill Zitek of the tsunami warning center in Palmer said the quake hit at 11:21 a.m. Alaska time in a sparsely populated area about 20 to 40 miles east of Kobuk, or 260 to 280 miles northwest of Fairbanks.
