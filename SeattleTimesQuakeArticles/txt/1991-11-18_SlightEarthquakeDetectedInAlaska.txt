ANCHORAGE     A moderate earthquake struck Alaska early yesterday. There were no reports of damage or injuries.     The temblor measured 4.4 on the Richter scale and hit at 3:06 a.m. Alaska time, said Charlotte Rowe of the Alaska Earthquake Information Center in Fairbanks.     It was a "deep" earthquake, centered about 60 miles underground, about 100 miles west of Kodiak, Rowe said.
