PALMER, Alaska - A  moderate earthquake jolted the Anchorage area last night, but no damage was reported.     The quake, which struck at 8:53 p.m., Pacific Daylight Time, registered a preliminary magnitude of 4.5 on the Richter scale and was centered 40 miles northwest of Anchorage beneath a largely uninhabited area called Susitna Flats, Alec Medbery of the Alaska Tsunami Warning Center said
