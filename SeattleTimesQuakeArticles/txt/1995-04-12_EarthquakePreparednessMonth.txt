EDMONDS - Just in case you missed it, city officials throughout Snohomish County are reminding residents that April  is Earthquake Preparedness Month.     Edmonds, Bothell, Mountlake Terrace, Lynnwood, Brier, Mill Creek and Woodway have issued proclamations to recognize the event and will conduct several activities to inform residents how to live through a quake.     The Emergency Services Coordinating Agency is helping cities plan for a number of activities, which will culminate with an earthquake drill on April 26.     Cities, school districts, the Red Cross, hospitals, utility companies and other organizations are expected to participate. Officials want residents to prepare emergency supplies that could help them survive for at least three days after an earthquake
