Culinary-arts students at Seattle Central Community College will donate more than a week's worth of school-restaurant tips to victims of the earthquake in Kobe, Japan.     The school has two student-run restaurants, one formal and the other casual. Students usually pool their tips to buy needed equipment for the culinary-arts program, but on Tuesday they voted to donate tips received from yesterday through Feb. 3 to the earthquake victims, said college spokesman Everett Reagan.     By coincidence, the formal restaurant is featuring Japanese cuisine this week
