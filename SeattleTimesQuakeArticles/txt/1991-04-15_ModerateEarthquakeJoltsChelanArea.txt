CHELAN, Chelan County     A moderate earthquake, registering 4.0. on the Richter scale, rattled residents near this Central Washington town but caused no reports of injuries or damage.     The quake struck yesterday at 10:30 p.m., said Rick Benson, a research scientist at the University of Washington Geophysics Center in Seattle. It was centered about 14.5 miles north-northeast of Chelan, he said.     Moderate quakes like the one last night have periodically been recorded in the Central Washington area between Entiat and Chelan, Benson said. Chelan has a population of about 2,800.
