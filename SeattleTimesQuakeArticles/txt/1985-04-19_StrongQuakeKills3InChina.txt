KUNMING, China (AP) _ A severe earthquake jolted China's southwest province of Yunnan, killing three people, injuring 10 and causing damage to homes, news reports said today.        The official English-language China Daily, published in Peking, said the quake yesterday registered 6.3 on the Richter scale. It said some houses were destroyed and communiciations disrupted.     Officials said it was the second earthquake in Kunming in 11 years.         The jolt occurred at 1:52 p.m. and lasted about 10 seconds, with its epicenter located near Kunming, the China Daily reported.
