Medical personnel are hastily being mobilized and supplies gathered by Northwest Medical Teams, an emergency relief organization based in Salem, Ore., to respond to victims of the earthquake that struck Mexico yesterday         One four-member team, including two trauma specialists, flew from Seattle to Mexico City today to make a survey of victims' needs and set up treatment facilities.         A spokesman for Northwest Medical Teams said more medical personnel were being recruited, and he urged people to respond to the emergency in Mexico with donations of money and such supplies as antibiotics and bandages.         Donations may be made by calling 1-800-845-900 toll-free.         Ron Post, director of the organization, and an interpreter were among the team that left here this morning on a Mexicana Airlines flight.
