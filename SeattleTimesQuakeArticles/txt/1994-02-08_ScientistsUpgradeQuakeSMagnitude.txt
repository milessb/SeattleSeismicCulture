LOS ANGELES - The California Institute of Technology and the U.S. Geological Survey said yesterday they were upgrading the magnitude of the Jan. 17 earthquake from 6.6 to 6.7, based on their own analyses and other digital seismographs from around the world    An official announcement of an upgraded magnitude is also expected this week from the National Earthquake Information Center in Golden, Colo., a Geological Survey spokeswoman said.
