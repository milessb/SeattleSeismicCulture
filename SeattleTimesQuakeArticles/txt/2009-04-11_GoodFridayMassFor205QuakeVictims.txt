Relatives of earthquake victims pay tribute before the start of an emotionally charged state funeral Mass in L'Aquila, Italy, where 205 coffins were arrayed in a vast courtyard. The Mass was televised as Italy marked a national day of mourning for the nearly 300 victims of Monday's earthquake.
