VANCOUVER A strong earthquake occurred this morning beneath the Pacific Ocean about 150 miles off Vancouver Island, the U.S. Geological Survey reported.    Scientists at the USGS National Earthquake Information Service in Golden, Colo., said early seismograph readings indicated the tremor registered 6.5 on the Richter scale. It occurred at 6:55 a.m. PDT.    The USGS said the earthquake was felt in the Port Hardy area near the northern end of Vancouver Island.
