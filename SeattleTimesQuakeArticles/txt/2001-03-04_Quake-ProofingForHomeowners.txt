For free information, including a list of contractors qualified to do retrofitting, call Project Impact at 206-615-0288 or visit its Web site:       www.cityofseattle.net/projectimpact      . Homeowners also can sign up for a two-hour class, sponsored by the Phinney Neighborhood Association, that teaches home retrofitting. Call 206-789-4993. If enough contractors express interest, University of Washington Extension Education may continue its six-hour course for contractors. Developed in cooperation with Seattle Emergency Management and the UW Department of Construction Management, the noncredit course teaches approved methods of quake-proofing older, wood-frame houses. Those who complete the program are added to the list of approved contractors. More information:  206-543-2310.&nbsp;
