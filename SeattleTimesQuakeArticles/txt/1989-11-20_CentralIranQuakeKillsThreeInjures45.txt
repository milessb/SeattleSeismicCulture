NICOSIA, Cyprus - A powerful earthquake jolted central Iran today, killing at least three people and injuring 45 others, the national news agency IRNA said.        The casualties occurred in the village of Golbaf after the earthquake measuring 6.0 on the Richter scale shook Kerman province bordering the central Iranian desert.        Golbaf and the nearby town of Shahdad, both east of the provincial capital Kerman, bore the brunt of the quake.
