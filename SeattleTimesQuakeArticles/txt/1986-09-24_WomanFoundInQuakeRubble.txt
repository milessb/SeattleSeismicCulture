ATHENS _ A 75-year-old woman was pulled from the debris of her collapsed house 10 days after a strong quake hit the southern Greek city of Kalamata, killing 20 people, the state-run radio reported today     The woman's desperate cries finally caught the attention of a passer-by.     Rescuers found her in the debris surrounded by her cats and some chickens she kept in the garden of her house, Greek newspapers said today.     The survivor was brought to Kalamata hospital for treatment, the radio said.
