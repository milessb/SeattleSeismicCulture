FAIRBANKS, Alaska      A moderate earthquake centered just north of the entrance to Denali National Park rattled parts of interior Alaska yesterday.      The earthquake measured 4.6 on the Richter scale.      No injuries or damage were reported.      The quake, at 6:02 a.m., was felt in Healy, McKinley Village and Igloo Service near Cantwell.      The quake woke some people on the south side of Fairbanks, according to the Alaska Earthquake Information Center in Fairbanks.
