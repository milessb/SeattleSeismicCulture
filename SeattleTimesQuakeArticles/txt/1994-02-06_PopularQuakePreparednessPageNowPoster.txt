The Seattle Times has now published a poster-quality version of our recent earthquake-preparedness page in response to requests from readers.     The posters will be available for 25 cents each, including tax, if you pick them up at a Seattle Times office.   Individual copies and small quantities are available from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. at The Seattle Times' downtown and suburban offices.     Call 464-2461 for information about mail and bulk orders.    The downtown office is at Fairview Avenue and John Street.     The others are 10777 Main St., Suite 100, Bellevue; 31620 23rd Ave. S., Suite 312, Federal Way; and 1211 164th St. SW, Suite 101, Lynnwood.
