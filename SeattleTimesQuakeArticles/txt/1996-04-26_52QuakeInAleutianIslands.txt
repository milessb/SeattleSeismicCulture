PALMER, Alaska  - A moderate earthquake was reported in the Aleutian Islands this morning, but there were no injuries or damage, the Alaska Tsunami Warning Center said.    The quake, with a preliminary magnitude of 5.2, occurred at 4:06 a.m. It was located 55 miles southeast of Nikolski, in the Aleutians, about 375 miles east of Adak, said the center's Guy Urban. The quake was felt strongly at Nikolski, Urban said.
