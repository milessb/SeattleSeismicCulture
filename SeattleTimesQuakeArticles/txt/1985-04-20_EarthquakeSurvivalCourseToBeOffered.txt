A course to help families prepare for a major earthquake will be held at 7 p.m. April 30 at the American Red Cross headquarters, 1900 25th Ave. S.         Called ``Safety and Survival in an Earthquake,'' the course includes information on what to do before and after a quake hits, home emergency supplies, first aid, water purification, instructions for turning off utilities, and how to make the home safer.         An advance registration of $5 per person is required, and families are encouraged to attend. The registration fee includes a book on the training.         Interested parties should call Red Cross Disaster Services at 323-2345.
