PALMER, Alaska - A 5.3 magnitude earthquake rattled a remote area in the Aleutian Islands yesterday morning, the Alaska Tsunami Warning Center said. There were no reports of injuries or damage.    The quake, at 6:53 a.m., was  45 miles northwest of Amchitka Island, or 220 miles west of Adak Naval Air Station. There are no permanent inhabitants on Amchitka.
