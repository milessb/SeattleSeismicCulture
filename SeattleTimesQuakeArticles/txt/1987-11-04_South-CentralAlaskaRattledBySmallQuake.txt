PALMER, Alaska _ A small earthquake rattled south-central Alaska, but there were no reports of damage or injuries, the Alaska Tsunami Warning Center reported.         The temblor at 3:33 p.m yesterday measured 4.4 on the Richter scale and was centered about 10 miles southwest of Palmer.         The Richter scale is a measure of ground motion as recorded on seismographs. Every increase of one number means a 10-fold increase in magnitude.
