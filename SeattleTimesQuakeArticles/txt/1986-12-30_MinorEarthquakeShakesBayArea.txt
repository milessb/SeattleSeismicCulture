SAN FRANCISCO _ A minor earthquake shook the San Francisco Bay area yesterday, making itself felt from Salinas north to San Rafael, but causing no reported damage or injuries.        The state Office of Emergency Services estimated the quake's magnitude at 3.5 on the Richter scale. The temblor was centered about 5 miles east of Milpitas, about 45 miles southeast of San Francisco, according to Emergency Services spokesman Lloyd Barrington.
