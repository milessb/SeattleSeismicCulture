SEQUIM, Clallam County - An earthquake with a magnitude of 3.4 shook the area near here yesterday, the University of Washington seismology laboratory said.     The quake occurred at 12:27 p.m. beneath the Strait of Juan de Fuca, about six miles southwest of Friday Harbor.     A Port Angeles radio station reported it received calls from listeners who felt the shaking in the Sequim area and Port Angeles. There were no reports of damage.     Seismology-lab spokesman Bill Steele said there also was a smaller quake, measuring 1.6, at 12:37 p.m. near Victoria,  B.C.
