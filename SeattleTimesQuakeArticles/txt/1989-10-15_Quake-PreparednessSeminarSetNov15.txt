A free seminar on what area residents can do before, during and after an earthquake will be sponsored by Highline Community Hospital from 7 to 9 p.m. Nov. 15 in the hospital cafeteria, Riverton Campus, 12844 Military Road S        The speakers will be Dr. Jack Keating, a University of Washington psychologist; Carole Martens, Washington State Earthquake Preparedness coordinator, and Gary Furlong of the Pacific Rim Disaster Team.         Seating is limited. For reservations, call 246-0511, Monday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.
