ORCAS, San Juan County     A mild earthquake struck this Orcas Island town yesterday morning, but there were no reports of injuries or damage, officials said.     The quake measured 2.8 on the Richter scale and occurred at 9:57 a.m. said Seth Moran, a graduate student at the University of Washington Geophysics Center in Seattle. It was centered near Orcas Island, about 20 miles northeast of Victoria, B.C., and about 26 miles west-southwest of Bellingham.     "There are quakes that occur in the northwestern part of Washington, but they're usually a little farther south," Moran said. "This is the first one I know of in quite a while that occurred near the San Juans.
