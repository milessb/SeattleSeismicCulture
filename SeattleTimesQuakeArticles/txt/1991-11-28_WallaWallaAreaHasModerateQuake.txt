WALLA WALLA     An earthquake registering 4.0 on the Richter scale occurred shortly after 5 p.m. in the area of Walla Walla and Milton-Freewater, Ore., the University of Washington seismology center reported yesterday     KNDU-TV in the nearby Tri-Cities said it received a number of calls from the Walla Walla area reporting the quake. There were no immediate reports of damage.     The Richter scale is a measure of ground motion as recorded on seismographs. An earthquake of 4.0 on the Richter scale can cause moderate damage
